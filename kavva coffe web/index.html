About Us

Our Coffee Journey

Kavva Bangladesh was founded in 2015 with a vision to revolutionize Bangladesh's coffee culture. What began as a small passion project has grown into Dhaka's premier specialty coffee destination.

The Kavva Philosophy

We believe that great coffee starts with great beans. That's why we travel the world to source the finest single-origin beans from sustainable farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, Brazil, and Indonesia.

Our Commitment to Quality

Every batch of beans is roasted in-house using our custom-built roaster to highlight unique flavor profiles. Our baristas are trained in international standards to ensure every cup meets our high expectations.

More Than Just Coffee

We've created a community space where coffee enthusiasts gather to learn, share, and enjoy exceptional brews. From coffee workshops to cultural events, we're building connections one cup at a time.
